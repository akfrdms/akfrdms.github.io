Privacy Policy for EMOJI BOMB

Last updated: 2026-03-11

EMOJI BOMB (“the App”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting users’ information.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or precise location. However, we use third-party advertising services that may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Identifiers: Such as the Google Advertising ID (AAID).
  • Basic Device Information: Necessary to display advertisements and ensure app functionality.

2. How We Use Information

The information collected is used solely for:

  • Displaying personalized or non-personalized advertisements through AdMob.
  • Ensuring proper functioning of advertising services and ad measurement.
  • Preventing fraud and maintaining security.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service:

Google AdMob (Google Ireland Limited / Google LLC): AdMob may collect and process data such as device identifiers to provide advertising services. For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: Google’s Privacy & Terms

4. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell, trade, or rent users’ personal information. Data is shared only with Google AdMob for advertising purposes. All data transmitted by third-party services is protected using secure, encrypted connections (HTTPS).

5. User Rights and Data Control

Since the App does not use a separate account system, we do not directly store or manage personal data on our servers.

Advertising ID: Users can reset or delete their Advertising ID at any time through their device settings (Settings > Google > Ads > Delete advertising ID).

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will promptly delete it.
